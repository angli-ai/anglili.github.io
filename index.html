Biography

I am a Principal Scientist at Baidu Apollo, building a safe, scalable and lifelong AI ecosystem for autonomous driving.

Until 2021, I was a Staff Research Scientist at Google DeepMind, where I researched the directions toward making artificial intelligence more scalable and applicable to the real world. I worked closely with Google teams on deploying the latest AI technologies into Alphabet products such as Waymo, Youtube, Google Maps, Google Cloud, and Google Ads. While I was in the Google headquarters, I spent quite a bit of my time engaging and advising AI technology with startups (e.g., WattTime) as a Partner Mentor at Google for Startups. Prior to that, I researched artificial intelligence at CMU Robotics Institute, Apple, Google Streetview, Facebook AI Research, and Comcast Labs DC. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science in 2017 from the University of Maryland College Park, and B.S. from Nanjing University.
